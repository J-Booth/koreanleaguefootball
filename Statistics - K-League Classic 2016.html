Championship and Relegation Split

The top 6 and bottom 6 teams are split for the remaining 5 games, with the top 6 teams competing for the title and the bottom 6 competing to avoid relegation. This split will occur from round 34. The team with the most points in the championship group will be delcared champions whilst the team which finishes bottom in the relegation group will be relegated to the K-League Challenge. The team which finishes 11th overall (2nd bottom) in the K-League Classic will face the winner of the K-League Challenge promotion play-offs in a 2-legged tie to determine if they will be relegated to the K-League Challenge or remain in the K-League Classic for the following season.
