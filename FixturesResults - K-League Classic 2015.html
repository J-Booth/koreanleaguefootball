Promotion-Relegation Playoffs

The promotion-relegation playoffs are held between the winners of the K-League Challenge playoffs and the K-League Classic team which finishes 11th. The 2 teams play each other twice with each team playing a home and away match. If the aggregate score is level by the end of the second leg the teams will play an addtional 30 minutes of extra time and should no team capitalise during this time the outcome of the playoffs will be decided by a penalty shootout. The eventual winner will earn a place in the K-League Classic for the following season.

Suwon FC (K-League Challenge playoff winners) and Busan I'Park (finished 11th in K-League Classic) will compete in the 2015 promotion-relegation playoffs.
